About Us

Custom Parties by Bobbie was founded 26 years ago by Bobbie Lonsk. Bobbie has a bachelor's degree in art education and an associate's degree in apparel design. The goal of Custom Parties is just that, to customize your party to suit your vision, your lifestyle, as well as your budget.

Just ask the hundreds of satisfied clients and you will hear words like:

  • "...easy and a pleasure to work with."
  • "...the party was everything we had hoped it would be!"
  • "...the room looked absolutely fabulous."
  • "...Wonderful!...Awesome!"
  • "Thank you for creating such a beautiful setting, it was perfect."

Bobbie provides a unique way of seeing that you get the party you envisioned that makes your day stress free.
